Thank You to UNC Groundskeeping

Spring Semester 2019

Throughout the semester, ACT participated in various events such as the RHA Duke vs. UNC basketball
viewing party, and students had the opportunity to write thank you letters to UNC Groundskeeping
staff. Our Groundskeepers work so hard every day to keep our campus clean, safe, beautiful, and
welcoming. Especially during the recent hurricanes and snow storms, they worked hard to clear roads,
walkways, and fallen debris. Thank you to all the staff at UNC Grounds Department!! Your amazing
work does not go unnoticed!

Hug A Heel Week

2/19/18-2/23/18

ACT collaborated with RHA to host Hug A Heel Week to promote acceptance and kindness around campus,
in the spirit of Valentines Day. A social media campaign to hug a fellow Tar Heel (#hugaheelweek)
promoted interaction between students, faculty, professors, friends, and strangers. Students wrote
candy grams to friends on campus and wrote letters to their favorite teachers.

Soda Tab Collection for Ronald McDonald House Charities

November 2017

November 2017 Throughout November 2017, ACT hosted a dorm competition for collecting soda tabs
(any tabs on the tops of soda cans, soup cans, veggie cans, or other aluminum cans) for the Ronald
McDonald House Charities. The dorm floors with the most soda tab donations won Insomnia cookies.
Overall, we collected almost 9,000 soda tabs!

Ronald McDonald House Charities Donated soda tabs get recycled, helping the environment,
and all funds generated from recycling the soda tabs go directly to the Ronald McDonald House Charities
to offset housing costs. This charity buys and renovates houses close to hospitals in order for low-income
family members to stay in cost-free housing close to their loved ones rather than sleeping in waiting room chairs.
There are currently more than 350 houses worldwide.
